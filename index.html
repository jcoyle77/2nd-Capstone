Customer Reviews

Dafran

I really enjoyed my experience with "Rank Up"! I've played the game Counterstrike: Global Offensive on and off, but when Riot Games recently released their new FPS called Valorant, I wanted to try this new game. I was decent at it, but I realized there was still a lot that I could improve in. So, when I found out about Rank Up, I was very excited about finding the opportunity I'd been looking for. The coach that I worked with was "Dafran". His expertise in the FPS, or first person shooter genre of games was extremely helpful. One of the first things he reccomended was to lower my mouse sensitivty, since hitting the targets requires keen precision. This required me to get a larger than average mouse mat, but when I did this I did indeed see a large improvement in my aim and accuracy. Additionally, he also reccomended and aim trainer called "Kovaak 2.0". It has many different aim drills for improving accuracy. By doing this every day for however much I can tolerate, whether its 15 minutes or an hour, etc, my aim has been improving significantly. I plan to continue practicing what Dafran taught me. I'm very satisified with the service he provided through Rank Up!

Sodapoppin

I have played the MMO World of Warcraft casually on and off for about five years now. I struggled to achieve a high rank in pvp (player vs player). That's when I found out about Rank Up! I enlisted Sodapoppin's services, and he taught me how to have more mastery over the class I play. Sodapopping has been playing World of Warcraft for roughly fifteen years. His vast knowledge of the game, and how each class plays, etc. has made him a very good coach. He taught me the optimal rotations (which of my classes abilities to use when), how to break line of sight on an enemy by taking advantage of moving behind pillars when they try to attack me. He taught me to interupt their abilities near the end to avoid being faked out by a cast that they might cancel.
